## Why a blog

I've been building software for a while now. Most of what I learn disappears into Slack threads, commit messages, and conversations that nobody can search later. This blog is where I'll write things down properly.

Expect posts about:

- **AI agents** and the engineering problems that come with building them
- **System design decisions** and the tradeoffs behind them
- **Things I got wrong** and what I learned from it

## What I won't do

I won't write tutorials that rehash documentation. If something is already well-explained elsewhere, I'll link to it. The posts here will focus on decisions, tradeoffs, and the parts that documentation doesn't cover.

> The best writing comes from having an opinion and being willing to defend it.

## The stack

This blog runs on MDX files in the repo, rendered by Next.js at build time. No database, no CMS, no admin panel. Write markdown, push to git, done.
